User guidance

How to fill the data correctly:

  • The first name and last name must match your official name as listed on your NRC or GUSTO College Student ID.
    For example, if your name is Htet Oo Wai Yan, your first name should be Htet Oo Wai and your last name should be Yan.
  • Your nickname can be anything you like, but remember it will appear on your profile.
